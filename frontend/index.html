🔬 What Are V1–V28?

Original Data

Transaction time, amount, merchant type, location, card usage patterns, and more — containing sensitive customer info.

After PCA Transform

28 uncorrelated principal components (V1–V28) that preserve variance while fully anonymizing personally identifiable data.

Why It Matters

V14 and V10 alone account for ~59% of the model's fraud detection power — fraud transactions show dramatically different patterns in these components.
